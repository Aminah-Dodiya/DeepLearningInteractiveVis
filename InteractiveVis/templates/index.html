The interactive relevance map visualization app displays brain areas that contribute to the model’s decision. For the task to dectect Alzheimer’s disease, high relevance (shown in red) corresponds to disease-specific gray matter volume reduction (atrophy).

Disclaimer: This app is not intended to be used for clinical use and decision making. Although the models achieve a high average accuracy, they are still subject of research. This app is provided for illustration purpose only.
